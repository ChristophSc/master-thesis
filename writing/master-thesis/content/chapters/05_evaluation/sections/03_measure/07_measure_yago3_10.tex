\textbf{YAGO3-10 Dataset}
\label{subsubsec:metrics_yago3_10}\\
%
Looking at the development of evaluation metrics MRR and Hit@10 on the biggest among all used datasets \textsc{YAGO3-10}, an improvement until the end of training can be seen (\Autoref{fig:advtrain_metrics_yago3_10}).
In addition, all model pairs perform equally well with a slight increase the whole time.
The highest improvement is at the beginning of training until 100 epochs, afterward only  small improvements follow.
Furthermore, all uncertainty measures perform equally well.

Even if there is a difference in the values of the rewards for different discriminator models, their course is identical (\Autoref{fig:advtrain_metrics_yago3_10_rew}).
This can be recognized for all uncertainty measures.
