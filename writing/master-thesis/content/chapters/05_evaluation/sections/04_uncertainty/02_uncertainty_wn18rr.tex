\label{subsec:uncertainty_wn18rr}

\Autoref{fig:advtrain_wn18rr_random_vs_uncertainty}
- course of MRR values on \textsc{WN18RR} dataset looks similar for both sampling methods \origsampling and \ussoftmax 
- in both sampling methods maximum MRR value is reached after about 200 epochs of MRR of 17.5\%.
- in comparison Hit@10 values constantly increase for both sampling methods such that maximum of about 42\% is reached at 1000 epochs
-> further learning with more epochs can be expected
