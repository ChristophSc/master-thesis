In this chapter the new approach of \usgan is evaluated.
For this purpose, \Autoref{ch:evaluation:sec:experimental_setup} first explains the experimental setup under which conditions and settings the evaluations are conducted.
Subsequently, the different uncertainty sampling methods \usmax and \ussoftmax are first evaluated in \Autoref{ch:evaluation:sec:evaluation_methods}.
Since several uncertainty measures are available, their results on different datasets are compared and evaluated in \Autoref{ch:evaluation:sec:evaluation_metrics}.
A final evaluation of the best sampling by uncertainty method is made in \Autoref{ch:evaluation:sec:evaluation_uncertainty} to compare it to the original sampling of \kbgan.
